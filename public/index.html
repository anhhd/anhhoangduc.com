About me

Anh Hoang Duc is a Business Analytics professional from Hanoi, Vietnam. He has a background in Quantitative Methods in Economics & is a co-founder of Ranalytics.vn, a group of data-addicted R users specializing in data analytics with the dream to bring the power of R & analytics to Vietnam. He is also an instructor of RStudio & Datacamp in Vietnam, providing various aspects of R such as data analysis, data wrangling, data vizualization or predictive modelling with R.

Currently, Anh is leading the Advanced Analytics department in Business Inteligence Competency Center in VPBank with the purpose to build a team with strong analytics capability to solve real business problems within the bank. The team has been applying various techniques to help the bank understand customers better.
